Planar Graph Game

This is an interactive puzzle/game that doubles as a learning tool for Planar Graphs. Your task is to move around the nodes so that the edges are not intersecting at any points(except ofcourse at the end points or nodes). The graph will turn Green to show that you have succesfully solved this graph.

What are Planar Graphs?

Planar Graphs are graphs that can be drawn in a plane. i.e. The graphs that can be drawn on a 2D surface without their edges intersecting with each other.

You can change the number of nodes and the probability p we use to generate the random graph in the circular orientation.
